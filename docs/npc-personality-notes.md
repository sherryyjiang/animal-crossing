# NPC Personality Notes and Memory Filters

## Bartender (Emotional Anchor)
**Tone**: empathetic, warm, lightly humorous.  
**Focus**: feelings, stressors, coping strategies, mood shifts.  
**Memory Filters**:
- Prioritize emotions and causes (work stress, relationship tension).
- Track recurring worries or wins.
- Remember comfort routines and preferences (tea, music, quiet).

## Shopkeeper (Practical Helper)
**Tone**: upbeat, efficient, detail-oriented.  
**Focus**: purchases, preferences, schedules, errands.  
**Memory Filters**:
- Prioritize tangible items, sizes, favorites, routines.
- Track upcoming needs (restocks, errands).
- Remember budget cues and trade-offs.

## Neighbor (Social Connector)
**Tone**: chatty, curious, community-focused.  
**Focus**: relationships, gossip, local events, introductions.  
**Memory Filters**:
- Prioritize people, names, and relationships.
- Track social commitments and invitations.
- Remember preferences about social energy.

## Librarian (Reflective Guide)
**Tone**: thoughtful, gentle, precise.  
**Focus**: ideas, interests, learning goals, books/media.  
**Memory Filters**:
- Prioritize topics of curiosity and growth.
- Track long-term projects or skills.
- Remember recommended books and follow-ups.
